THE ROLE

Quality through careful testing.

QA Testers work closely with the development team to identify defects, reproduce issues, verify fixes, and provide structured feedback on gameplay systems before updates reach players.

Loading the current QA opening…
01

Test systematically

Follow test plans and explore edge cases across combat, maps, UI, progression, equipment, multiplayer systems, and new content.

02

Report clearly

Document bugs using accurate reproduction steps, expected and actual results, severity, screenshots, clips, and device information.

03

Verify fixes

Retest resolved issues, check for regressions, and communicate whether a fix works under the conditions in which the problem occurred.

WHAT YOU WILL DO

  • Test private Roblox builds and upcoming ASTRYXIA content.
  • Identify gameplay, visual, UI, networking, and progression issues.
  • Submit organized bug reports through the studio’s assigned system.
  • Reproduce reported problems and verify completed fixes.
  • Provide practical feedback without replacing the design team’s direction.
  • Keep private builds, assets, discussions, and unreleased information confidential.

WHAT WE ARE LOOKING FOR

  • Strong attention to detail and patience when repeating test cases.
  • Clear written communication in English.
  • Ability to separate personal preference from an actual defect.
  • Reliable access to Roblox, Discord, and a computer capable of recording evidence.
  • Professional conduct and respect for confidentiality.
  • Previous testing, moderation, development, or technical experience is helpful but not required.
